About Me

Who am I?

Since a young age, I have been passionate about computers and programming. I started coding in elementary school for the purposes of hacking and modding video games, a passion which took me on a journey to learn from XML syntax to JAVA and C++. However, my vision of the future of apps is web based, which led me to learn PHP and JavaScript. Since then, I've established myself as a web-app developer focusing on both back and front ends, and developing AJAX intensive applications.
